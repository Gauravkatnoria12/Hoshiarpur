# 🏛️ Hoshiarpur — The City of Saints

Hey! 👋 This is the source for my Hoshiarpur city concept website — a premium, editorial-style digital tribute to one of Punjab's most culturally rich cities. Less "tourism brochure," more "art installation." 🎨

---

## 🌆 What Is This?

A single-page, immersive experience built to showcase the history, heritage, and landmarks of **Hoshiarpur, Punjab**. The design is inspired by the city's most iconic craft — the intricate wooden inlay work — and translates that sense of precision and artistry into a digital medium.

No frameworks. No npm. Just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript doing some really satisfying things. ✨

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎬 **Hand-crafted Loader** — An SVG geometric inlay pattern draws itself stroke-by-stroke while a progress counter ticks up to 100%
- 🖱️ **Custom Cursor** — A circle that inverts colors using `mix-blend-mode: difference` and expands on hover elements
- 🌓 **Day / Night Toggle** — Full dark/light theme switch via CSS custom properties, triggered by a single click in the header
- 🎞️ **Hero Entrance Animation** — The title text slices in from below, staggered line by line, after the loader exits
- 📜 **History Timeline** — A vertical timeline covering the 1300s right up to today, with large terracotta year markers
- 🚀 **Infinite Marquee** — Giant outlined/filled text scrolling horizontally — "HOSHIARPUR · PUNJAB · DOABA · CULTURE"
- 🗺️ **Landmarks List** — An interactive hover-list of key locations with name and category
- 🌾 **Film Grain Texture** — An SVG fractalNoise overlay for that tactile, analog feel
- 👁️ **Scroll Reveal** — Elements fade and slide up as you scroll into them via IntersectionObserver
- 📱 **Responsive** — Works cleanly on mobile too, with layout adjustments for smaller screens

## 🎨 Design Language

The whole aesthetic is built around **Hoshiarpur's identity as a city of precision and heritage:**

- **Cinzel** (the display font) — Roman-inspired, feels carved in stone, matches the ancient-meets-modern vibe perfectly
- **Terracotta accent** (`#c44d28`) — Pulled directly from the color of traditional wood inlay pigments
- **Noise overlay** — Adds a hand-pressed, printmaking feel to the smooth gradients
- **`mix-blend-mode: difference`** on the header and cursor — Creates that inversion effect without needing a separate dark-mode header style
- **Outlined marquee text** (`-webkit-text-stroke`) — A typographic technique that makes the scrolling section feel editorial and loud without being visually heavy
- **Minimal color palette** — Warm off-white (`#e8e6e1`) in light mode, near-black (`#0f0f0f`) in dark mode, with terracotta as the only accent
